Research Interests

I am interested in developing recommendation systems that not only capture but actively elicit users’ latent intentions throughout their interaction history. My research focuses on leveraging multimodal signals to more clearly reveal and represent these hidden preferences, ultimately enabling more accurate and explainable recommender models.

In parallel, I also work on multimodal retrieval, aiming to represent the intrinsic characteristics of each modality and align them effectively so that a user’s query can be matched with the most relevant document.
